Some frequently asked questions... Let's answer them

What is the Project? ⬆

The project calculates the calories burned during exercises using factors like exercise type, duration, intensity, and personal metrics such as weight.

What is average Heart Rate? ⬆

Heart rate is the number of times your heart beats per minute, with the average resting heart rate for adults typically ranging between 60 to 100 beats per minute.

Are the calorie estimates suitable for all fitness levels? ⬆

The calorie estimates provide a general guideline and are suitable for most fitness levels, but individual variations in metabolism and physical condition can affect accuracy.

How do I lose weight using the calorie calculator? ⬆

To lose weight, you generally need to consume fewer calories than you burn. So, use the calculator to find the excercise best suited for you.

How do I gain weight using the calorie calculator? ⬆

To gain weight, you generally need to consume more calories than you burn. So, use the calculator to find the excercise best suited for you.
